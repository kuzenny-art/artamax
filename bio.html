artist bio

UP Date: January 4th, 2026

Leonid Kuzenny is an artist and architect whose work is rooted in a strong sense of form, space, and visual harmony. Known for his cityscapes, abstract compositions, and expressive graphic works, Kuzenny combines architectural precision with free artistic intuition, creating images that convey the atmosphere of modern cities and the inner rhythm of space.
His practice spans traditional painting, mixed media, and digital art, where he explores structure, balance, and contrast through a personal visual language. Drawing inspiration from urban environments, geometry, and everyday scenes, his works often reflect a dialogue between order and spontaneity. Now based in Germany, Leonid Kuzenny continues to develop his artistic voice, creating both physical canvases and digital artworks. His pieces are presented on online platforms and are held in private and public collections.
